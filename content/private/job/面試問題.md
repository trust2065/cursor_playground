問我的Next.js經驗

While Next.js is famous for SEO and static rendering, I've found its value in SaaS company lies in full-stack consistency.

In my previous projects, I used monorepo with Zod to share type through full-stack. Changes made are transparent to both frontend and backend. I also found Server Actions useful - it simplified form handling and data mutations.

I also like the middleware to handle Auth and centralise the role-base access control. Duplicating role logic is a pain to maintain when the app scales.
